Demonstration Projects Notice

The projects in my Portfolio are self-initiated demonstration projects. Every design decision, every line of code and every brand system in them is my own real, professional work — created to show what I can build for clients.

Because these are concept projects, the brand names in them (such as "MyBank" and "Evergreen Learning Studio") are invented. They are not real companies, nothing can be purchased through them, and any product details such as prices or interest rates are examples only. Any similarity to real brands or persons is unintentional.

My website, my qualifications, my experience and my services are, of course, entirely real — get in touch and see for yourself.

Privacy Policy

This website respects your privacy and is designed to collect as little information as possible, in line with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

What is collected: nothing automatically. This site has no user accounts, no analytics trackers and no advertising. The demo forms inside fictional projects do not send or store any data.

If you contact me: the contact form opens your own email program — your name, email address and message come to me only as a normal email. I use these details only to reply to you. I do not share, sell or add them to any mailing list. You may ask me to delete our correspondence at any time by emailing tulaytsambardasdesign@gmail.com.

Hosting: like every website, the hosting provider may keep standard technical server logs (such as IP address and time of visit) for security. I do not access or use these for profiling.

Cookie Policy

This website does not set cookies, and it does not use analytics, advertising or social media tracking technologies. Because no cookies are used, no cookie consent banner is required.

If cookies or analytics are ever added in the future, this policy will be updated first and consent will be requested where required by law.

Accessibility Statement

I aim for this website to be usable by everyone, including people who use assistive technologies. The site is built with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2, level AA as its target: semantic HTML landmarks, text alternatives for images, sufficient colour contrast for text, keyboard-accessible navigation, visible focus, and reduced-motion support.

Some pages inside older fictional demo projects may not yet fully meet this standard; they are being improved over time.

If you experience any barrier using this site, please tell me at tulaytsambardasdesign@gmail.com — I will do my best to fix it or provide the content in another form.

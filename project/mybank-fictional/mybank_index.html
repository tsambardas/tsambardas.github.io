First Home Buyers Guide

3.4 %

Interest

Buying your first home is one of the biggest steps you will ever take. Our First Home Buyers Guide walks you through every stage, from saving a deposit to getting the keys.

Fixed introductory rate of 3.4% p.a. for eligible first home buyers.

How we help you get started

Our home lending specialists explain how much you could borrow, what your repayments might look like, and which government grants and schemes you may qualify for as a first home buyer.

Start with a free 30-minute appointment at your nearest branch, by phone, or by video call. We will help you understand your deposit options, pre-approval, and the costs involved in buying — such as stamp duty, inspections and legal fees — so there are no surprises on settlement day. When you are ready, your dedicated lender stays with you through the whole application, and our guide keeps you on track with a simple step-by-step checklist you can follow at your own pace.

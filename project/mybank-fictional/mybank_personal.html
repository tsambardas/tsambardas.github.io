Debit accounts

Debit account interest 3.5% p/m

Bank your way, every day.

Our everyday debit account comes with no monthly fees, unlimited electronic transactions and a Visa Debit card you can use worldwide. Earn bonus interest simply by depositing your salary and keeping your balance above $500 each month.

A card that works as hard as you do.

Tap and pay with your phone or watch, shop online safely with real-time fraud monitoring, and withdraw cash fee-free at over 3,000 ATMs across Australia. If your card is ever lost or stolen, lock it instantly in the app and order a replacement in seconds.

Open your account online in under ten minutes — all you need is your ID and an Australian residential address. Your digital card is ready to use straight away, while the physical card arrives within five business days.

Saving accounts

Whether you are saving for a holiday, a home deposit or a rainy day, our savings accounts reward regular deposits with bonus interest. Set a goal in the app, automate your transfers, and watch your progress grow month by month.

Retirement rates

Higher fixed rates for customers aged 55 and over, so your retirement savings keep working while you relax.

Choose a term that suits your plans — from three months to five years — and lock in your rate from day one. Interest can be paid monthly into your everyday account to top up your income, or reinvested to grow your balance faster. There are no setup or account-keeping fees, and our retirement specialists can help you split your savings across terms so money is always available when you need it.

  1. 6-month term — 4.10% p.a.
  2. 12-month term — 4.45% p.a.
  3. 24-month term — 4.70% p.a.

All about you

Everyone saves differently. That is why our savings specialists take the time to understand your goals before recommending an account, a term deposit, or a mix of both.

Book a free savings review in any branch or by phone. We will look at how you spend, how much you can comfortably put aside, and which bonus-interest conditions you can realistically meet — then build a simple plan you can stick to. Review your plan any time in the app, and adjust your goals as life changes. No jargon, no pressure, just clear guidance.
